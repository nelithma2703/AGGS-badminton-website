About AGGS Badminton

AGGS Badminton is a student-led sports club fostering passion, growth, and teamwork through badminton. Whether you're new to the sport or a seasoned player, we provide a fun, inclusive, and competitive environment for all.

What We Offer

  • 🏸 Weekly training sessions
  • 🎯 Intra-school tournaments
  • 🤝 Team building activities
  • 🥇 Leaderboard rankings
  • 🌟 Open to all year levels

Badminton Rules

Whether you're smashing in singles or rallying in doubles, knowing the rules ensures fair and fun play.

üè∏ General Rules

  • Games are played to 21 points (rally scoring).
  • Win by at least 2 points (up to max 30).
  • If the shuttle touches the ground, the rally ends.
  • No double hits or carrying the shuttle.
  • Only one service attempt is allowed per point.

üë§ Singles Rules

  • Play is 1v1.
  • Use narrow and long court boundaries.
  • Serve from the right side when your score is even, left if odd.
  • Each rally scores a point, regardless of who served.

üë• Doubles Rules

  • Play is 2v2.
  • Use wide and short court for service; wide and long for rallies.
  • Each pair gets only one service turn before rotating.
  • Players alternate who serves based on position and score.

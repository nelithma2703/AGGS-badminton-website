RULES

Scoring System

  • Matches are played best of 3 games to 21 points.
  • Every rally counts (rally scoring system).
  • If the score reaches 20–20, a player/pair must win by 2 clear points (e.g., 22–20, 23–21).

Serving

  • The serve must be hit underhand and below the server’s waist.
  • The shuttle must travel diagonally across the court into the opponent’s service box.
  • In singles: serve from the right when the score is even, and from the left when it’s odd.

Gameplay

  • A rally begins with a serve and continues until the shuttle hits the ground, goes out, or a fault occurs.
  • A player may only hit the shuttle once per side.
  • The shuttle must not touch the ceiling, walls, or a player’s body/clothing.

Faults

  • If the shuttle lands outside the court boundaries.
  • If the shuttle does not cross the net.
  • If a player touches the net with their body or racket.
  • If a player hits the shuttle twice in one rally.

Winning a Match

  • The first player/pair to win 2 games wins the match.
